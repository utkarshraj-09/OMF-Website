Our Work

At MITTI Foundation, we are committed to creating sustainable change through various initiatives that focus on education, cultural preservation, entrepreneurship, and community development. Our work is driven by the belief that every individual deserves access to quality education, cultural enrichment, and opportunities for growth.

Education

Transforming the future of under-resourced schools by upgrading infrastructure, providing quality education, and creating an environment conducive to learning.

Learn More

Dharohar

Celebrating and preserving India's rich cultural legacy through art, music, and traditional practices, ensuring they are passed on to future generations.

Learn More

Entrepreneurship

Empowering individuals with entrepreneurial skills and mentorship to create sustainable livelihoods and contribute to economic development.
