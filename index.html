Welcome to Our MITTI Foundation

Our MITTI Foundation is a not-for-profit organization working to build a more inclusive and empowered society. We focus on grassroots initiatives in education, entrepreneurship, culture, and social development, aiming to create self-sustaining models that uplift communities across India.

Our Mission

Building Sustainable Change through Education, Entrepreneurship & Engagement.

Our Vision

Empowering People. Strengthening Communities. Preserving Heritage.

Our Values

Amplifying Voices. Nurturing Talent. Inspiring Action.
